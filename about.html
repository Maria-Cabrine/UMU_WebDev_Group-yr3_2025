Our Story

Welcome to Marimart, your destination for curated lifestyle essentials.

Marimart was founded in November 2024 by Maria Cabrine, from whom the store proudly takes its name. What started as a passion for bringing modern, aesthetic designs to everyday life has grown into a beloved local brand.

Located in the heart of Kayabwe, we are dedicated to serving our community with products that elevate your home and personal style. Whether you are looking for the perfect decor piece or a stylish accessory, Marimart is here to inspire you.

Thank you for being part of our journey.
